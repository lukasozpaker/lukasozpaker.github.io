HOW IT WORKS

About the Business and Investment Club

WHAT WE DO

We meet every Thursdays at lunch to discuss the world of finance and invest through our stock market game, which mirrors the real market, but with fake money. At the end of the year, the top 3 with the most % gain will receive prizes.

HOW TO JOIN

You can join at any time. If you just want to check business and investment out, just show up and have a seat. To join talk to any member of the leadership so we can get you started on the stock market game and register you for the club. We look forward to seeing you!

NEW THIS YEAR

This year, we're taking part in a statewide stock market game with the possibility of winning a 25k scholarship.
